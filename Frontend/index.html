Services

Overview

We are specialized in a wide range of services, including External Audits, Tax Advisory, Tax Computation & Declaration, Financial Consultancy, Accounting Services, Financial Reporting, Feasibility Study, Mergers & Acquisitions, and Internal Control Review.

Whether you are a startup, a small and medium sized entity (MSME), a large corporation or a non-governmental organization (NGO) in need of comprehensive audit services and seeking guidance, A.M.C. is committed to provide the expertise and support necessary for your success.
