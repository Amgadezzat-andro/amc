Welcome to our Internship Program!

We are excited to announce the launching of A.M.C.'s Internship Program for students and graduates who are eager to gain practical experience and jumpstart their careers.

Why Join?

Our program offers a unique opportunity to:

  • Gain Hands-On Experience
  • Learn from Experts
  • Build Skills and Networks
  • Bridge Education with practice

Program Highlights

Duration

Our 8-week internship program offers flexible schedules to help interns balance work and study.

Mentorship

Each intern is paired with a mentor who will provide guidance, support and feedback throughout the program.

Professional Development

Interns have the opportunity to attend when available workshops, training sessions, and networking events to enhance their career prospects.
